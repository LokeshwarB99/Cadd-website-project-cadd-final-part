Overview

World-Class Education Worldwide

Established in 1987, CADD Centre Training Services Private Limited, an ISO 9001:2015 certified, Asia's biggest network for CAD Training, offers multi-disciplinary training delivery to individuals in over 25 countries.

With more than 1000+ franchise outlets across all those 25 countries in the last 31 years, CADD Centre has outshined in training over 1.5 Million engineers, architects, graphic artists and management professionals, who are now successfully employed in key positions in large and small organizations in more than 50 countries.

CADD Centre Chennai is the training arm of the 31 years old CADD Centre Group, headquartered in Chennai, India.

CADD Centre is the one stop solution for your CAD Training needs. We are specialized in Computer Aided Design (CAD), Computer Aided Engineering (CAE) and Computer Aided Manufacturing (CAM), with our wings spread across the world.

CADD Centre provides training courses which are industry-specific which have immediate relevance and use in the industrial world and productivity gain. Our courses are designed to provide a good volume of knowledge and a high degree of skill.

In Chennai, our major training institutes are exclusively located in Anna Nagar, Velachery & Padi.

CADD Centre is also the preferred training partner of more than 6500 corporate houses in India and abroad. Our training methodology is very well appreciated by corporate. With CADD improved productivity of the trained professionals is apparent. CADD Centre also offer customized training programs based on your specific requirement.

ITA

CADD Centre believes, Quality is King. And ITA is integral in CADD Centre's instructional excellence. CADD Centre's Internal Training Academy (ITA) comprises of CAD experts, thoroughly monitor our training programs & keep a regular check on quality. Faculties are re-certified at a regular interval. ITA is responsible for imparting professional training to CADD Centre instructors countrywide to enhance their efficiency and product knowledge.

These in-house teams regularly keep in touch with every centre across the country to know their requirements and to facilitate suitable product training. Only ITA certified instructors are allowed to lead a classroom. With such strict practices, you can feel relaxed and you will be assured to develop skill sets that are most relevant and industry specific.

Certification

On completion of every course at CADD Centre, a course completion certification is endowed featuring the name of the student, the program enrolled, details of the particular center and the topics covered. And a Quick Response (QR) code will be provided to prove the authenticity of the certification which can be verified online with the student identification number.
